> There are flowers, and there are weeds.
> 
> One is beautiful and blossoms, while the other is invasive and harmful.
> 
> One is to be cherished, yet the other is to be removed by the root—to stop the growth permanently.
> 
> People are like flowers and weeds—cherish the flowers, remove the weeds.
