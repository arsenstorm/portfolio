---
title: Impermanence.
date: 2024-12-28
---

> In its presence lies beauty—yet we cling to it, fear it, and futilely try to control it.

---

Everything is cherished more as it fades—this is the bittersweet beauty of impermanence.

Yet, it is human nature to hold on to what was, fear what will be, and _desperately_ try to mold the inevitable into something we can control.
