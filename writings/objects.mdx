> All physical elements—living or not—are objects.
>
> This begs the question, how do you compliment someone on their appearance without ‘objectifying’ them?

---

Can physical beauty be admired without diminishing one’s humanity?

We’re animate, breathing, living beings—unlike the inanimate objects around us—yet, _we’re still objects_.

How, then, do we compliment someone’s beauty without reducing them?
